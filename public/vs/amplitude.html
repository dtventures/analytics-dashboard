What is Amplitude?

Amplitude is a product analytics platform designed for teams building apps and digital products. It tracks user events — button clicks, feature usage, checkout flows — to help product managers understand behaviour, build funnels, and measure retention.

It is not a web traffic analytics tool. You won't see organic search performance, keyword rankings, or referral sources in the way GA4 does. Amplitude requires you to instrument your product with their SDK and define custom events before you see any data.

⚠️ Amplitude replaces your tracking — it does not read GA4. Migrating means re-tagging your entire site or app with Amplitude's SDK. Any historical GA4 data stays in GA4.

What is AnalyticsDash?

AnalyticsDash reads your existing GA4 and Google Search Console data and presents it in a clean, fast dashboard — no new tracking required. Connect once with Google and your historical data is immediately visible.

It also ships a CLI tool so you can check your traffic, top pages, and keyword rankings directly from your terminal or inside AI tools like Claude Code — without ever opening a browser.

💡 Already using GA4? AnalyticsDash gives you a better view of your existing data in two minutes. No migration, no re-instrumentation, no SDK.

When Amplitude makes sense

Amplitude is the right choice if you're building a SaaS product or mobile app and need deep user-journey analysis: funnels, cohorts, retention curves, and A/B testing at scale. It's built for product teams with engineers who can instrument events.

When AnalyticsDash makes sense

AnalyticsDash is the right choice if you run a website, blog, or content business and want to see your traffic, SEO performance, and keyword rankings without wading through GA4's complex interface — or if you want those metrics available in your terminal.

Bottom line

Different tools for different jobs

Amplitude and AnalyticsDash are solving completely different problems. Amplitude is overkill — and the wrong tool — for most websites and blogs. If your data is already in GA4, AnalyticsDash gives you a faster, cleaner view of it in minutes, for free.
