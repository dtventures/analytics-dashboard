Which tool should you pick?

The right tool depends entirely on what you're trying to measure — and how much time you want to spend setting it up.

✓ Choose AnalyticsDash if...
You already have GA4 and want more from it
You run a website, blog, or content business. You already have GA4 installed and just want a faster, cleaner view — plus Search Console keyword rankings and a terminal CLI. Setup takes two minutes and costs nothing.
Choose Amplitude if...
You're building a product and need user journey analytics
You have a SaaS app or mobile product and need funnels, cohort retention, A/B testing, and deep user-event analysis. You have engineers who can instrument custom events. Budget and setup time are not constraints.
Choose Plausible if...
Privacy is your top priority and you want to leave GA4
You want to drop GA4 entirely, avoid cookie consent banners, and run a lightweight GDPR-compliant tracker. You're comfortable starting fresh with no historical data. $9/month is fine.
Choose Umami if...
You want full data ownership and love self-hosting
You're a developer who wants complete control over your data with no third-party involved. You're comfortable running a Node.js + database stack. You want open-source and don't mind managing your own infrastructure.
